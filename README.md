Barcode Essential is an essential app for every Android device.
<br><br>
Scan barcodes on products, or Data Matrix and QR Codes containing URLs, contact info, etc. You can easily scan the barcodes and copy that data directly to your clipboard.
<br><br>
There is also a built in barcode generator in the app. You can generate your own barcode by using that feature and save it directly to you gallery.
<br><br>

<br>
The supported barcode types are:
<br>
- QR_CODE<br>
- UPC_A<br>
- UPC_E<br>
- AZTEC<br>
- CODABAR<br>
- CODE_39<br>
- CODE_93<br>
- CODE_128<br>
- DATA_MATRIX<br>
- EAN_8<br>
- EAN_13<br>
- ITF<br>
- MAXICODE<br>
- PDF_417<br>
<br>
In Barcode Generator you can now only generate QR Code. Rest of the code generation options will be added in future updates.
<br><br>
<b>Notes:
In order to use Scan, your device must have a built-in camera. To scan product barcodes, your device must have autofocus.</b>
